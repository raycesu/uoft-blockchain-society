What we do

Whether you are new to blockchain or already building, UofT Blockchain Society helps you learn, connect, and create.

Learn

Intro and deep-dive sessions on blockchain fundamentals, smart contracts, decentralization, and emerging Web3 topics.

Build

Hands-on workshops, mini-hackathons, and project groups where members ship real blockchain-powered applications.

Connect

Meet fellow students, researchers, startups, and companies working in crypto, DeFi, and Web3 across Toronto and beyond.

Who can join?

Any University of Toronto student (undergrad or grad), from any faculty, campus, or background. No prior blockchain experience required.

  • CS, engineering, math, and data science
  • Business, economics, and finance
  • Law, policy, and social sciences
  • Artists, designers, and anyone curious
