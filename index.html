Who can join?

Any University of Toronto student (undergrad or grad), from any faculty, campus, or background. No prior blockchain experience required.

  • CS, engineering, math, and data science
  • Business, economics, and finance
  • Law, policy, and social sciences
  • Artists, designers, and anyone curious
