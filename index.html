What we do

Whether you are new to blockchain or already building, we help you learn, connect, and create.

Learn

Intro-friendly and advanced sessions on blockchain fundamentals and Web3.

Build

Hands-on workshops and project groups to build real applications.

Connect

Meet students, researchers, startups, and industry teams across Toronto.

Partner companies & sponsors

We collaborate with blockchain, fintech, and digital asset organizations for events, mentorship, and recruiting.

Want to work with us? Visit the contact page to get in touch about sponsoring or hosting an event.

Who can join?

Any University of Toronto student (undergrad or grad), from any faculty, campus, or background. No prior blockchain experience required.

  • CS, engineering, math, and data science
  • Business, economics, and finance
  • Law, policy, and social sciences
  • Artists, designers, and anyone curious

Stay in the loop

Join our mailing list and Discord to hear about events, workshops, and opportunities.

  • 2–4 events per month during term
  • No spam, just relevant updates
